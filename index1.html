Analyzing the trending videos on YouTube

This project analyzes the YouTube trending videos dataset. The visualizations in this page
will help content creators, looking to advertise or create content on/for YouTube,
in targeting the right videos, channels, and categories.

Get Started

What kind of videos trend in each region?

This visualization shows the categories and tags that trend in each region.

Click on the regions in the map to drill-down and see trending categories and tags for that particular region. Click the same region again to see the overall view.

How is the engagement of trending videos?

The following visualizations show the engagement rate of trending videos by region and category. Notice the correlation between likes, comments, and views.

Click the bubble of any region to see the engagement distribution (likes, comments, dislikes) for that region by category. Click the bubble again to see the overall view.

Which channels have trending videos?

The following visualizations show information about the channels that have the most trending videos on the basis of views or video count. Please select the basis, countries, and the number of channels that you wish to see.

Click the channel bar to see the distribution of trending videos by category and time for that specific channel. Click the bar again to see the default overall view.
